**TITLE:** AI-Powered Discussion Monitor and Action Item Tracker


**PROBLEM STATEMENT:**
As virtual meetings and online collaboration environments become increasingly common, maintaining structured, focused, and outcome-oriented discussions has become a major challenge. Important decisions and action items are often missed or not tracked properly.

Existing platforms such as Zoom, Microsoft Teams, and Google Meet do not provide real-time intelligent monitoring or automation to manage discussions effectively.


**OBJECTIVE:**
To develop an AI-powered system that can monitor discussions in real-time, keep conversations focused, and automatically extract key points and actionable tasks for better productivity and accountability.


**PROPOSED SOLUTION:**
The proposed system aims to:
- Monitor live meeting discussions.
- Detect off-topic conversations and distractions.
- Capture key points, decisions, and action items.
- Generate structured summaries.
- Improve overall meeting efficiency.


**UNIQUE SELLING PROPOSITION (USP):**
- Keeps meetings focused by detecting off-topic discussions.
- Automatically captures key points and action items in real-time.
- Identifies unnecessary interruptions and distractions.
- Provides clear post-meeting summaries.
- Reduces manual note-taking effort.
- Works alongside existing platforms like Zoom, Google Meet, and Teams.


**TARGET USERS:**
- Students and faculty involved in academic discussions.
- Corporate professionals such as project managers and team leads.
- Teams involved in frequent meetings and collaborations.


**USER INSIGHTS:**
- Meetings often go off-topic.
- Important points and decisions are frequently missed.
- Manual tracking is inefficient and error-prone.
- Existing tools do not provide intelligent monitoring.
- Users prefer an AI-based system to manage discussions.


**KEY FEATURES (PLANNED):**
